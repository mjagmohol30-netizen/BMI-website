Welcome to BMITrack!

BMITrack is a web-based platform that helps users calculate and understand their Body Mass Index (BMI).
It is designed for students and individuals to monitor their health easily.
Aligned with SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

Objectives:


To help users calculate BMI easily

- This system allows users to quicklyandconveniently compute their Body MassIndex.


To identify weight status(underweight, normal, overweight, obese)

- It helps users determine their weight category based on standard BMI ranges.


To promote healthylifestyleawareness

- The system encourages users tobemoremindful of their health and daily habits


To support students inmonitoringhealth

- It assists students in tracking andunderstanding their physical healthstatusovertime.
